Income Tax Return (ITR) Filing Guide

A professional, step-by-step guide to filing your ITR in India for AY 2025-26.

1

Collect Your Documents

  • PAN & Aadhaar Card
  • Form 16 (from employer), Form 16A/26AS (TDS statements)
  • Bank Statements & Investment proofs (for deductions)
  • Capital gains statements & Other income documents
2

Determine ITR Form

Choose the correct ITR form based on your income sources. Common forms like ITR-1 are for salary income, while ITR-3 is for business professionals. Refer to the table for details.

3

Go to the e-Filing Portal

Visit the official Income Tax e-Filing Portal and log in using your PAN. The online mode is the most common and convenient method.

4

Fill Your Details

Enter your personal information, detailed income from all sources, and claim all eligible deductions (e.g., under 80C, 80D) to reduce your tax liability.

5

Calculate & Pay Tax

The portal will auto-calculate your tax. Cross-verify it with Form 26AS. Pay any balance tax due or confirm your refund amount.

6

Verify Your ITR

This is a mandatory step. The easiest way is to e-verify instantly using an Aadhaar OTP. Your return is invalid until verified.

7

Receive Acknowledgment

Once verified, the IT Department will process your return. You'll receive a confirmation (ITR-V) via email. Any refund will be credited to your pre-validated bank account.

Key ITR Forms

ITR-1: For salary, one house property & other sources (up to ₹50 lakh).

ITR-2: For capital gains & foreign assets (no business income).

ITR-3: For income from business/profession.

ITR-4: For presumptive business income.

Dates & Penalties

Due Date: 31st July 2025 (for individuals not requiring audit).

Late Fee: Up to ₹5,000 under Sec 234F for filing after the due date.
