Income Tax Return (ITR)

File if you have salary, business, rental or capital gains.

  • ITR-1: Individuals with salary/pension
  • ITR-2: Salary + capital gains
  • ITR-3 / ITR-4: Businesses/proprietors

Due dates:

  • Individuals (no audit): 31st July
  • Business with audit: 31st October

Documents required: PAN, Aadhaar, Form 16, bank statements, P&L, investment proofs (80C).

TCS (Tax Collected at Source)

Collected by seller for specific goods/services (e.g., scrap, foreign tour packages, high-value motor vehicles).

Return: Form 27EQ (quarterly).
