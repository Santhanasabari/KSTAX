Income Tax Act, 1961 – Detailed Sections

  • Section 1: Short title, extent, and commencement of the Act.
  • Section 2: Definitions of key terms like Assessee, Person, Income, Previous Year, Assessment Year.
  • Section 3: Scope of total income (what incomes are taxable).
  • Section 4: Charge of income tax.
  • Section 5: Residential status and scope of total income.
  • Section 6: Determination of residential status.
  • Sections 7–10: Income deemed to accrue or arise in India.

A. Salaries (Sections 15–17)
  • Section 15: Salary includes wages, pension, gratuity, leave encashment.
  • Section 16: Deduction from salary (standard deduction ₹50,000 + professional tax).
  • Section 17: Perquisites and allowances (HRA, LTA, rent-free accommodation).
B. Income from House Property (Sections 22–27)
  • Section 22: Annual value of property is taxable.
  • Section 23: Determination of annual value.
  • Section 24: Deductions like home loan interest and 30% standard deduction.
  • Sections 25–27: Special cases (co-ownership, let-out property).
C. Profits and Gains from Business or Profession (Sections 28–44DB)
  • Section 28: Business income.
  • Section 32: Depreciation on assets.
  • Section 35: Scientific research expenses.
  • Section 36: Other allowable business deductions.
  • Section 44: Presumptive taxation for small businesses and professionals.
D. Capital Gains (Sections 45–55A)
  • Section 45: Capital gains on transfer of assets.
  • Section 48: Computation of capital gains.
  • Sections 54–54F: Exemptions on reinvestment in residential property.
E. Income from Other Sources (Sections 56–59)
  • Section 56: Income like gifts exceeding ₹50,000, lottery, interest on securities.
  • Section 57: Expenses allowed from other sources.
