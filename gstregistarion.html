Documents Required for GST Registration

Proprietorship / Individual
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Bank Account Proof (Cancelled Cheque)
  • Address Proof (Electricity Bill/Rent Agreement)
Partnership / LLP
  • PAN Card of the Firm/LLP
  • Partnership Deed / LLP Agreement
  • PAN & Aadhaar of all Partners
  • Photograph of all Partners
  • Bank Account Proof
  • Proof of Principal Place of Business
  • Authorization Letter for Signatory
Private / Public Ltd. Company
  • Company PAN Card
  • Certificate of Incorporation (MCA)
  • Articles & Memorandum of Association (AOA/MOA)
  • PAN & Aadhaar of all Directors
  • Company Bank Account Proof
  • Board Resolution for Authorized Signatory
