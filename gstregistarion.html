Quick facts

Who needs to register, and the commonly used thresholds and scheme limits (check local authority for updates).

  • Registration form: REG-01 (apply on GST portal)
  • GST registration is free: There is no government fee for registration — professional fees may apply if you hire help.
  • Thresholds overview: See the sections below for goods vs services and composition limits.
Apply on the GST portal (REG-01)

Verification is via PAN, Aadhaar, and documents. Typical processing for low-risk applicants is faster.

Types & Step-by-step

Regular Taxpayer (Normal)

Most businesses with turnover above the registration threshold. Need to file regular returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B etc.).

  1. Collect PAN, mobile, email — create GST portal account
  2. Fill REG-01 with business & promoter details
  3. Upload proof of business address, PAN, bank details, photos, and authorization
  4. Verification & ARN issuance — GSTIN is assigned on approval
Composition Scheme (Simplified for small taxpayers)

Small taxpayers can opt for a composition scheme to pay tax at a fixed rate and enjoy simpler returns. Restrictions apply on interstate sales and certain businesses.

  • Turnover limits (general): Traders & manufacturers up to ₹1.5 crore; Services up to ₹50 lakh (special states lower limits may apply).
  • Register as composition dealer via REG-01 and opt into composition on the portal or during registration.
Partnership / LLP

Partnership firms must provide partnership deed, PAN of firm (if any), and PAN/Aadhaar of partners and an authorized signatory.

  1. Ensure PAN of partnership firm (or PAN of partners for proprietorship-like cases)
  2. Upload partnership deed, address proof, bank proof, and KYC of partners
Private Limited & Public Limited Companies

Companies must register with company PAN, incorporate documents (MoA/AoA), directors' KYC, authorized signatory details and bank account proof.

  • Provide incorporation certificate, PAN of company, list of directors with PAN/Aadhaar
  • Board resolution or authorization for authorized signatory (if required)

Common Documents Checklist

Identity & KYC
  • PAN of applicant / entity
  • Aadhaar of authorized signatory(s)
  • Passport-size photo of proprietors / partners / directors
Business & Address proofs
  • Business registration / incorporation certificate (for companies)
  • Partnership deed (for partnership)
  • Electricity bill / municipal khata / property tax / rent agreement + NOC
  • Bank account statement or cancelled cheque
Other notes & pointers
  • Government GST registration itself has no official fee; professional charges may apply if you hire a consultant.
  • Composition dealers cannot make interstate supplies (with exceptions) and cannot collect tax from customers as normal taxable person does.
  • Thresholds and special state limits change occasionally — verify with your state tax department or the official GST portal.
