Guide to Digital Signature (DSC)

A complete, step-by-step guide to applying for, verifying, and using your Digital Signature Certificate for all major government portals in India.

View the Process Required Documents

What is a Digital Signature?

DSC serves as your secure, legally valid electronic signature, equivalent to a physical handwritten signature.

Digital Identity

It authenticates your identity electronically, ensuring that online documents are verified and secure.

High Security

DSCs use strong encryption, making signed documents tamper-proof and legally protected under the IT Act, 2000.

Wide Acceptance

Mandatory for GST, Income Tax, MCA (ROC), e-tendering, PF, and other government portals.

Documents Required

The application process requires standard KYC documents for both individuals and organizations.

For Individuals
  • PAN Card copy
  • Aadhaar Card copy (front & back)
  • Active Mobile Number & Email ID
For Organizations
  • All documents listed for Individuals
  • Company/Firm PAN & Registration Proof
  • Authorization Letter for the signatory

The DSC Application Process

Follow these simple steps to obtain your Digital Signature Certificate and USB token.

Step 1
Choose Provider & Type

Select a licensed Certifying Authority (e.g., eMudhra, Sify) and choose a Class 3 DSC with your desired validity (1-3 years).

Step 2
Online Application & Document Upload

Fill out the online form with your PAN/Aadhaar details and upload the required documents. Aadhaar eKYC via OTP is the fastest method.

Step 3
Complete Video Verification

Follow the link sent to your mobile/email to complete a short live video verification, where you will state your name and show your original PAN/Aadhaar.

Step 4
Approval & Download

Once the provider approves your application, you will receive a confirmation. You can then download the DSC into your secure USB token.

Common Uses of DSC

DSC is a versatile tool required for compliance across various government and business portals.

GST Returns Income Tax e-Filing MCA/ROC Filings e-Tendering EPFO Portals Import/Export (DGFT)
